13 Virtual Experiences that will amaze you

Museums

  1. One of largest art museums in the world, the Louvre that is located in Paris, France, offers free online tours of four exhibits, including Egyptian Antiquities. Tap here for more information.
  2. The Smithsonian Natural Museum of Natural History hosts the largest natural history collection worldwide (more than 145 millions of specimens) and provides with a 360 degree-room by room virtual tour for each and every exhibit. Tap here for more information.
  3. The Vatican Museum in Rome, offers in total 7 virtual tours among which the most famous Capella Sistina, Saint Peter’s Basilica and Raphael’s room. Tap here for more information.
  4. The MoMA, located in New York city, provides online virtual tours on numerous exhibitions. Tap here for more information.
  5. For fashion lovers, the Boston Museum of Fine Arts, is an ideal choice as among the offered virtual tours, one section dedicated to the 21th century Designer Fashion is available. Tap here for more information.

Ways to travel the world

  1. Explore the scenic wonders in five U.S. National Parks: the Kenai Fjords in Alaska, Hawaiian volcanoes, New Mexico’s Carlsbad Caverns, Utah’s Bryce Canyon, and Florida’s Dry Tortugas. Tap here for more information.
  2. Smithsonian magazine offers a collection of virtual travel experience, digital exhibits, and books that will transport you to far-off lands.Tap here for more information.
  3. New York times has chosen 52 beautiful and interesting places all around the world for our enjoyment, such as the rocky peaks of Glacier National Park. Tap here for more information.
  4. Travel to Napa Valley and observe the nature with its breathtaking views, along with a peaceful flock of sheep. Tap here for more information.

Ways to observe animals and nature

  1. Smithsonian National Zoo in Washington, D.C. allows you to observe a variety of animals such as a cheetah and her new cubs, giant pandas Tian Tian and Mei Xiang, 6 Asian elephants, the lion habitat, and a colony of naked mole rats. Tap here for more information.
  2. With 11 live camera options San Diego Zoo allows you to observe a wide variety of animals, including koalas, polar bears, condors, and tigers. Tap here for information.
  3. From the comfort and safety of home, watch horses in Kentucky, a tropical reef aquarium in California, the Northern Lights in Canada, pandas in China, beautiful sunsets in Hawaii, and more. Tap here for more information.
  4. New England Aquarium has created virtual tours for anyone who is interested about finding out more about their animals. Tap here for more information.
