Keep in mind...

Don't forget to take safety measures to protect yourself. That's why we have written a few tips to help you out.

Protect yourself and your loved ones by following these tips:

  1. Wash your hands often.
  2. Avoid close contact.
  3. Cover your mouth and nose with a mask when around others.
  4. Cover coughs and sneezes.
  5. Clean and disinfect surfaces.
  6. Monitor your health daily.
  7. Know how it spreads.
